destructor

A destructor is a method that has the same name as its class, but proceeded by a tilde mark(~). A destructor is executed at the time of destroying the object. The most important use of destructor is to deallocate the memory that was allocated for the objects by the constructor.

note

destructor has no return data type

#inclide<conio.h>
